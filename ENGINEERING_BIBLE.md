Minimal.

Invisible.

Fast.

Apple-level experience.

# Design Language

Apple Human Interface Guidelines.

Black and White.

Large Typography.

Glass effects only where necessary.

Rounded corners.

Soft shadows.

No unnecessary gradients.

Animations under 300ms.

Motion should communicate—not decorate.
